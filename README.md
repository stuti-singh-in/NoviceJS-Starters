# NoviceJS-Starters

Welcome to NoviceJS-Starters repository! 🚀 This collection is your gateway to the fascinating world of JavaScript. Whether you're a complete beginner or just dipping your toes into coding, we've got something fun and educational for you!

## Explore the Projects:

### 1. Detect_Device_Battery
- **Description:** Feeling curious about your device's battery level? This project lets you detect and display it with a touch of magic! 🧙‍♂️

### 2. Pacman
- **Description:** Get ready for some nostalgia and fun with Pacman! Control your little yellow hero through a maze of challenges. 🕹️

### 3. PasswordGenerator
- **Description:** Tired of using "password123"? Level up your security game with this handy password generator! 🔒

### 4. QR code Generator
- **Description:** Embrace the future and generate QR codes for URLs and text. Share information in a snap! 📲

### 5. Simple_XO_Game
- **Description:** Unleash your competitive spirit with the classic XO game (Tic-Tac-Toe)! Challenge a friend or play against the clever AI. ❌⭕

### 6. Translator_App
- **Description:** Language barriers? Not anymore! Translate text effortlessly with this nifty app. Namaste! 🗺️
